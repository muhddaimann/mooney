# 🍽️ Mooney — Split a group dinner in seconds

**Never do "who owes what" math again.**
Tap the food & drinks you ordered, and Mooney works out your share — service charge and tax included.

👉 **https://mooney-one.vercel.app/**

---

## Why

After a group dinner, working out each person's share is a hassle — totals, 10% service charge, 6% tax, and everyone arguing over the receipt. Mooney turns that into a few taps.

## The full flow

1. **Sign in** — pick your role to enter the app.
2. **Open the Menu tab** — the restaurant's items, grouped by Mains / Sides / Desserts / Drinks, each with its price.
3. **Tap what you ate** — adjust quantities with − / +. A running line at the bottom shows what you've selected.
4. **Next → enter your name.**
5. **See what to pay** — Mooney shows:
   > This is how much you need to pay, **{name}**
   > **RM ──.──**
   …with the subtotal, **service charge (10%)** and **service tax (6%)** broken out.
6. **Pay & record** — scan the QR to pay, then tap the link to tick that you paid and how much, straight into the shared sheet.
